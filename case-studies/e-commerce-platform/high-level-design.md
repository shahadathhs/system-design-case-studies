# High-Level Design

The system will consist of the following microservices:

1.  **User Service:** Manages user authentication, authorization, and profiles.
2.  **Product Service:** Manages product information, categories, and search.
3.  **Inventory Service:** Tracks product stock levels.
4.  **Cart Service:** Manages user shopping carts.
5.  **Order Service:** Manages order creation, tracking, and history.
6.  **Payment Service:** Integrates with payment gateways and handles transactions.
7.  **Recommendation Service:** Provides personalized product recommendations.
8.  **Review Service:** Manages product reviews and ratings.
9.  **Notification Service:** Sends email, SMS, and push notifications.
10. **API Gateway:** Entry point for all client requests, handles routing, authentication, and rate limiting.
11. **Frontend Applications:** Web and mobile clients.
12. **Databases:** Various databases suitable for each service's needs.
