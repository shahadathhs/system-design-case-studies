# Requirements

## Functional Requirements

*   Users can browse products by category, search, and view product details.
*   Users can add products to a shopping cart.
*   Users can proceed to checkout, provide shipping information, and make payments.
*   Users can view their order history.
*   Sellers can list products, manage inventory, and view orders.
*   Admin users can manage users, products, and orders.

## Non-Functional Requirements

*   **High Availability:** The platform must be highly available, especially during peak shopping seasons.
*   **Low Latency:** Product browsing, search, and checkout processes must be fast.
*   **Scalability:** The system should be able to handle a large number of concurrent users, products, and orders.
*   **Consistency:** Strong consistency for critical data like inventory and payment, eventual consistency for less critical data like product reviews.
*   **Security:** User data, payment information, and transactions must be secure.
