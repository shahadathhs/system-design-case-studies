# Requirements

## Functional Requirements

*   **Employee Management:**
    *   Store and manage employee personal details, contact information, job history, and roles.
    *   Support onboarding and offboarding processes.
*   **Payroll Management:**
    *   Calculate salaries, deductions, and taxes.
    *   Generate pay stubs and reports.
    *   Integrate with banking systems for direct deposits.
*   **Time and Attendance:**
    *   Allow employees to submit time-off requests (vacation, sick leave, etc.).
    *   Managers can approve or reject time-off requests.
    *   Track employee work hours.
*   **Reporting:**
    *   Generate various HR reports (e.g., headcount, salary reports, time-off summaries).
*   **Access Control:**
    *   Different user roles (employee, manager, HR admin, payroll admin) with specific permissions.

## Non-Functional Requirements

*   **Security:** High-level security for sensitive employee and payroll data.
*   **Data Integrity:** Ensure accuracy and consistency of all HR data.
*   **Scalability:** Ability to manage data for thousands of employees.
*   **Availability:** High availability for critical HR functions, especially payroll.
*   **Compliance:** Adherence to labor laws, tax regulations, and data privacy (GDPR, CCPA).
*   **Auditability:** All changes to sensitive data must be auditable.
